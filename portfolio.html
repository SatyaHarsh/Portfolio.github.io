My Projects

Project-1:Wireless Charging of Electric Vehicles

With the surge in popularity of electric vehicles (EVs), efficient charging solutions have become paramount. This project presents a cutting-edge approach to address this need through wireless charging technology. Unlike traditional plug-in methods, this system utilizes resonant inductive coupling to transfer power wirelessly from the charging pad on the ground to the vehicle's receiver. The prototype achieves a commendable efficiency rate, ensuring rapid charging comparable to conventional methods. Moreover, the design is scalable, accommodating various vehicle sizes. The integration of advanced safety mechanisms guarantees minimal electromagnetic interference and ensures user safety. This technology not only promises convenience by eliminating the need for physical connectors but also paves the way for dynamic charging possibilities, such as charging while driving. The success of this project could revolutionize the EV infrastructure, propelling us closer to a cleaner and more sustainable future.

Project-2:Solar Dryer and Solar Still

The increasing demand for sustainable solutions in water purification and food preservation led to this project's conception, focusing on a combined solar dryer and solar still system. Capitalizing on abundant solar energy, the system serves a dual purpose: drying agricultural produce and distilling water. The solar dryer utilizes a series of trays positioned to maximize exposure to sunlight, ensuring efficient evaporation of moisture and preservation of the produce. Simultaneously, the solar still component employs a basin design where impure water is heated by sunlight, causing it to evaporate and then condense on a sloped surface, leading to collection points. This dual-system not only addresses food wastage through effective drying but also offers potable water, essential in arid regions or areas with limited clean water access. The project's innovative approach promises to augment sustainability in agriculture and potable water production using eco-friendly means. This abstract offers a succinct overview of the solar dryer and solar still system, emphasizing its benefits, functionality, and potential impact.
